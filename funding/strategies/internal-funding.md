# Internal Funding Options

Wake Forest University funding opportunities for research leaves and related expenses.

## Reynolds Research Leave

### Overview

The Reynolds Research Leave provides faculty with dedicated time for focused research and scholarly work. Available every 5 years to eligible faculty members.

### Financial Support

- **Semester Leave**: Full salary coverage (100%)
- **Year-long Leave**: Two-thirds salary coverage (2/3 pay)
- **Teaching Load**: Typically taken during 2-course semester or 2-2 course year

### Application Requirements

See [Leave Instructions](../institutional/leave-instructions.md) for complete requirements and criteria.

## Faculty Development Grants

### Coverage Options

- **Full Salary**: Available for semester-long leaves
- **Partial Salary**: Covers 2/3 salary for year-long leaves
- **Research Expenses**: May cover associated research costs

### Strategic Alignment

Faculty development funding prioritizes projects that:
- Advance Wake Forest's strategic framework goals
- Demonstrate potential for significant scholarly impact
- Align with departmental and institutional priorities
- Show clear publication or dissemination plans

WFU's [IS Strategic Plan 2025](../institutional/leave-instructions.md) emphasizes "AI integration across the university" and positions Wake Forest "at the forefront of educational innovation."

**Potential Support Areas**:

### Teaching & Academic Excellence Funding

- **Faculty Commons**: Collaborative support for curriculum design and technology integration
- **Academic Technology**: Partnerships for educational innovation projects
- **Research Support**: Infrastructure for data management and analysis

## Department-Level Support

### Spanish Department Resources

- **Course Release**: Coordination for reduced teaching load
- **Student Participants**: Access to Spanish language students for research
- **Curriculum Integration**: Collaboration opportunities for system testing
- **International Programs**: Potential connections with study abroad contexts

### Institutional Research Collaboration

- **IRB Support**: Ethics review and approval processes
- **Data Collection**: Support for student assessment and evaluation
- **Statistical Consultation**: Analysis support for research outcomes

## Competitive Internal Grants

### Faculty Research Grants

- **Small Grants**: Typically $1,000-$5,000 for preliminary research
- **Large Grants**: Up to $15,000-$25,000 for substantial projects
- **Travel Grants**: Conference presentation and collaboration funding
- **Equipment Grants**: Technology and infrastructure purchases

### Cross-Disciplinary Initiatives

- **Digital Humanities**: Collaborative funding for technology-enhanced research
- **Teaching Innovation**: Grants for pedagogical development projects
- **Community Engagement**: Funding for projects with local/regional impact

## Timeline and Coordination

### Academic Year Planning

- **Application Deadlines**: Typically March for following academic year
- **Review Process**: Faculty committee evaluation with administrative recommendation
- **Coordination**: Alignment with departmental teaching schedules and sabbatical rotations

### Multi-Year Planning

- **5-Course Year Adjustment**: Reynolds leave can shift planned 5-course year to following academic year
- **Sequential Opportunities**: Planning for sabbatical followed by course reduction
- **Long-term Projects**: Coordination with multi-year research initiatives
